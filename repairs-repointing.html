Brick Repairs & Repointing Specialists

Deteriorating mortar joints and damaged brickwork aren't just an aesthetic problem — left unattended they can lead to water ingress, structural movement and costly damage. SN Brickworks provides skilled repair and repointing services that protect your property and restore its appearance.

We work on all types of properties from Victorian terraces to modern commercial buildings, matching mortar mixes, profiles and brick types to ensure repairs are sympathetic and long-lasting.

Repointing

Full repointing of brick and stone facades, walls and chimneys. We match mortar colour, mix and profile to complement the existing brickwork.

Brick Replacement

Individual and section brick replacement where damaged, spalled or eroded bricks need to be removed and replaced to match the existing.

Chimney Repairs

Chimney stack repointing, flaunching, rebuilding and capping. We inspect, advise and carry out all chimney brickwork safely and professionally.

Parapet Walls

Parapet wall repairs, rebuilding and capping — important structural work that protects roofs and building envelopes from water ingress.

Commercial Maintenance

Planned and reactive brickwork maintenance for commercial property managers, landlords and estate management companies.

Period Properties

Soft lime mortars and traditional methods for listed buildings and period properties where modern cement mortars would cause damage.
